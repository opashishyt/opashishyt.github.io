What is FRP or (Factory Reset Protection)?

Factory Reset Protection is an Android device security feature introduced by Google for Android 5.1 Lollipop and onwards. It provides extra protection for Android devices, especially for those unfortunate instances where you misplace or lose your device and someone else resets it.

FRP prevents unauthorized access to your device after a factory reset. You need to log in using your Google account to activate your device.

FRP triggers when:

  • You do a factory reset without removing the Google account first.
  • You forget your screen lock and do a hard reset to remove it.
